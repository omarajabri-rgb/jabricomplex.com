Who We Are

Jabri Industrial Trading Complex is a professionally managed industrial and commercial complex located in Amman. It was established in 1990 with a clear vision by Mr. Adnan M’K Jabri to support small and medium-sized businesses by providing practical, well-organized, and accessible industrial and commercial spaces.

Since its establishment, the complex has served more than 300 individuals and businesses, offering a stable and reliable environment for long-term operations across various sectors.

Ownership & Management

In 2002, Omar A. Jabri joined the operation of the complex and became directly involved in its day-to-day management and development.

In 2013, Omar A. Jabri assumed full responsibility as Owner, Chief Executive Officer, and Manager of Jabri Industrial Trading Complex.

This continuity in ownership and leadership has ensured clear management, consistent standards, and a strong understanding of tenants’ operational needs over the years.

Our Vision

To create a reliable industrial and trading complex that supports business growth through practical spaces, clear management, and long-term partnerships.

This vision continues to guide how the complex is owned, managed, and maintained today.

La Brioche

La Brioche is a family-run chocolate and confectionery business developed and operated within the Jabri business ecosystem. The brand reflects hands-on experience in manufacturing, product development, and day-to-day operations, combining traditional craftsmanship with practical, modern business practices. Through its growth, La Brioche represents a real, working example of how businesses are built, managed, and scaled—shaped by direct involvement in production, logistics, branding, and market execution.
