About Jabri Industrial Trading Complex

Jabri Industrial Trading Complex is a professionally managed industrial and commercial complex located in Amman. Established in 1990, Jabri has supported small and medium-sized businesses by providing reliable, accessible, and well-organized business spaces for long-term operations.

Over the years, Jabri Industrial Trading Complex has served more than 300 individuals and businesses, offering a stable and secure environment across multiple business sectors.

Jabri Ownership & Management

In 2002, Omar A. Jabri joined the operation of the complex and became directly involved in its daily management and development.

In 2013, Omar A. Jabri assumed full responsibility as Owner, Chief Executive Officer, and Manager of Jabri Industrial Trading Complex.

Jabri’s continuity in ownership and leadership ensures consistent standards, professional management, and a strong understanding of tenant needs.
