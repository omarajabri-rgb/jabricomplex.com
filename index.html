Supporting Small and Medium Businesses Since 1990

About the Complex

Jabri Industrial Trading Complex is a privately owned industrial complex located in Amman. It was established in 1990 with a clear vision by Mr. Adnan M’K Jabri to support small and medium-sized businesses by providing practical, accessible, and well-organized industrial spaces.

Over the years, the complex has served more than 300 individuals and businesses, offering a stable and reliable environment for long-term operations.

Ownership & Management

In 2002, Omar A. Jabri joined the operation of the complex and became directly involved in its day-to-day management and development.

In 2013, Omar A. Jabri assumed full responsibility as Owner, Chief Executive Officer, and Manager of Jabri Industrial Trading Complex.

This continuity in ownership and leadership has ensured consistent standards, long-term stability, and a deep understanding of the needs of businesses operating within the complex.

Our Vision

The complex was built on the belief that growing businesses need more than just space — they need an environment that supports efficiency, accessibility, and continuity.

This principle continues to guide how the complex is owned, managed, and maintained today.
